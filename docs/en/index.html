AuBookPlayer

A simple local audiobook player for Android.

Modified from the open-source music player Auxio to fit local audiobook listening.

Hard to believe, but Android lacked a genuinely good local audiobook player—either missing key features (skip intro/outro, playback speed, sleep timer), overly complex, or cluttered with ads. So I developed AuBookPlayer based on Auxio, designed specifically to play local audiobooks simply and easily.
